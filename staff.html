Description:


Mike started to ride bikes when he was 3 years old. As he grew up he would often watch his mother fix her bike and at the age of 12 started to work on his own bike in the garage. Mike woud eventually pursue a degree in civil engineering at Western University. After some time he would quit his civil engineering job for the government and start Bike Stop. Besides his passion for bikes, Mike dabbles playing video games, photography and thrifting. Mike can be quite stubborn but he means well and is more than happy to help anybody, no matter their issue.
