Welcome to Bike Stop!

Your one stop shop for most bike services



Bike Stop is a bike store in downtown Ottawa that welcomes everyone from bike finatics to people who have no clue about bikes. Bike stop offers a variety of services that will cater to any bike owner and their type of bike.
