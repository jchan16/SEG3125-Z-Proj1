Description:


Bike Stop has 4 repair benches you can book to use to repair your own bike. A repair bench has most of the tools you'll need to overhaul on your bike such as: grease tub, cone wrenches, screwdrivers, a vice, a bike stand, degreaser, rags, chain wrench and much more. When working at a work bench, you will be able to buy used or new bike parts if need be at an extra cost. Bike stop offers used and new: bearings, chains, tubes, tires, wheels, chains, gear or brake housing/wire, brakes, frames, derailers, cassettes and much more. For this service specifically, you dont need to choose a staff member on the booking page.

Description:


Bike Stop offers a repair service. Simply pick a time to drop off your bike and we'll email you when its done. The price and time of the repair will vary however you wont be charged more than $100. We accept all types of bikes including but not limted to: mountain bikes, road bikes, fold-able bikes, tri-cycles, uni-cycles etc. You can choose which Bike Stop staff to handle your repair but if you have no preference, simply pick the "No Preference" option when booking this service. We also offer a warranty of 2 weeks after a repair, to ensure quality of service.


Cost: < $100
